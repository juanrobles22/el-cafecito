Local Love

El Cafecito is a locally owned coffee shop established in 2023 and located in Zapata, Texas. Known for its creative specialty drinks, affordable pricing, and strong community atmosphere, the café blends modern coffee trends with Mexican-inspired flavors.


It has quickly become a local favorite, offering a mix of traditional coffee options and unique beverages like the Tres Leches Latte, Mazapán Latte, and Cajeta Latte — all served in a cozy, casual environment with indoor and outdoor seating.
